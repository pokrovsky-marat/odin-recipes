Boiled-eggs

Description

Boiled eggs are a simple and nutritious dish that can be enjoyed for breakfast, as a snack, or added to salads and sandwiches. They are easy to prepare and require only a few basic ingredients.

Ingredients

  • Eggs
  • Water
  • Salt (optional)

Steps

  1. Place the eggs in a saucepan and cover them with cold water.
  2. Put the saucepan on the stove and bring the water to a boil.

  4. Remove the eggs from the hot water and place them in cold water.
  5. Peel the eggs and serve.
