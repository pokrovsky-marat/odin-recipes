Fried Eggs

Description

A quick, reliable way to fry eggs: heat a skillet, add a little butter or oil, crack the eggs in, and cook until the whites are set and the yolks reach your desired doneness. Season simply with salt and pepper and serve hot.

Ingredients

  • 2 large eggs (per person)
  • 1 tbsp butter or neutral oil
  • Salt, to taste
  • Freshly ground black pepper
  • Optional: chopped parsley or chives

Steps

  1. Preheat a nonstick skillet over medium heat.
  2. Add butter or oil and swirl to coat the pan.
  3. Crack eggs into the pan (or into a bowl first, then slide into the pan).
  4. Cook until the whites are set; for sunny-side-up, do not flip.
