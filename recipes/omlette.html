Omlette

Description

An omelet is a light and fluffy egg dish that can be customized with different fillings like cheese, vegetables, or herbs. It is a great option for a filling breakfast or lunch.

Ingredients

  • Eggs
  • Milk (optional)
  • Salt
  • Black pepper
  • Butter or oil

Steps

  1. Crack the eggs into a bowl and whisk them until smooth.
  2. Add salt, pepper, and a small amount of milk if desired.
  3. Heat a pan over medium heat and add butter or oil.
  4. Pour the egg mixture into the pan and cook gently.
  5. Fold the omelet when set and cook for another minute.
  6. Serve warm.
